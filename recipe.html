Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. This authentic Roman recipe creates a luxuriously creamy sauce without any cream — just the magic of eggs and Parmesan. Perfect for a weeknight dinner that feels indulgent and satisfying.

30 min
Serves 4
Medium

  • Cooking Steps

  • 🌶️ to taste

Instructions

1
Boil the Pasta

Boil pasta in a large pot of generously salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

2
Fry the Pancetta

Meanwhile, fry the pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy and golden. Remove from heat to cool slightly.

3
Combine Everything

Combine cooked pasta with pancetta. Quickly toss with the egg and cheese mixture, using reserved pasta water to achieve a creamy consistency.

4
Serve & Enjoy

Serve hot with a generous amount of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and cracked black pepper. Buon appetito!
